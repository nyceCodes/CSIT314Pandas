How PandaClean Works

For Home Owners

  1. Search for nearby cleaners by service type and location.
  2. Review cleaner profiles, read ratings, and compare prices.
  3. Book your preferred cleaner and pay securely online.
  4. Enjoy a clean home and leave a review!

For Cleaners

  1. Create a profile with your services, prices, and schedule.
  2. Get matched with customers based on location and availability.
  3. Accept bookings and provide top-quality cleaning services.
  4. Receive payments and grow your reputation on the platform.

For Admins

  1. Monitor user activities and manage cleaner approvals.
  2. Review reports and platform analytics.
  3. Update service categories and pricing tiers as needed.
